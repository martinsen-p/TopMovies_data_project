# TopMovies_data_project

Here we analyzed the top IMDb Top 400 movies based on their runtime, popularity, and genre using kaggle datasets.


## Questions: 

Does a movie’s length affect its rank/rating? Do longer movies tend to score better, or is shorter and tighter the way to go? 

Is there a relationship between number of votes (popularity) and rating? Do "popular" movies actually rate higher, or do smaller films sometimes score better? 

What genre shows up the most? Has this changed over time?

## Our Findings:
We found that runtime does not have a strong effect on the rank of a movie. However, a higher popularity typically indicates a higher rank/rating. We also found that the most popular genre is currently action, although, this does change over time.
